CollegeCompass Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

CollegeCompass does NOT collect any personal information. The app works completely offline for rank prediction. No account creation required.

2. AI Compare Feature

When you use the AI College Compare feature, the names of the two colleges you select are sent to our secure backend (Cloudflare Worker) which forwards them to Google Gemini AI. No personal information is sent.

3. Data Storage

All KEAM cutoff data is stored locally on your device. We do not store any user data on our servers.

4. Third Party Services

We use Google Gemini AI for the college comparison feature. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect data from anyone including children under 13.

6. Contact

For any privacy concerns contact us at: adithgx@gmail.com
